Our Story

Petersen Cleaning & Junk Removal began in 2021 when Bobby and Allison petersen realized that Las Vegas needed a cleaning company that combined cutting-edge efficiency with a personal touch. What started as a one-team operation with a single vaccum has grown into a team of over 15 trained professionals serving both residential and commercial clients across the valley.

Our Mission

We believe a clean environment improves quality of life—whether it’s a small home, a bustling office, or a large retail space. Our mission is to provide eco-friendly, reliable, and affordable cleaning and junk removal services that our neighbors can trust. We’re not just here to clean; we’re here to build lasting relationships based on honesty, respect, and exceptional results.

Meet the Team

Every member of our crew undergoes background checks and thorough training before stepping into your home or business. Our supervisor, Sarah Lee, has over 10 years of experience in commercial facility maintenance, ensuring that we use the best industry practices. Meanwhile, Bobby leads our junk removal division, coordinating safe, eco-friendly disposal methods in compliance with Las Vegas recycling regulations.
