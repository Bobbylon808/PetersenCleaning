Our Story

Petersen Cleaning & Junk Removal began in 2021 when Bobby and Allison Petersen realized that Las Vegas needed a cleaning company that combined cutting-edge efficiency with a personal touch. What started as a one-team operation with a single vacuum has grown into a team of over 15 trained professionals serving both residential and commercial clients across the valley.

Why We’re Not Just Another Cleaning Company

At Petersen Cleaning & Junk Removal, our certified, licensed, and insured team delivers professional-grade results every time. We use eco-friendly, hospital-grade cleaning products to ensure a safe, healthy environment for your family or employees. Our punctual staff is committed to excellence, offering meticulous attention to detail and 100% satisfaction guaranteed. Choose us for reliable service, transparent pricing, and the highest standards in the industry.

Our Mission

We believe a clean environment improves quality of life—whether it’s a small home, a bustling office, or a large retail space. Our mission is to provide eco-friendly, reliable, and affordable cleaning and junk removal services that our neighbors can trust.
